How It All Started

Money Mind was born out of personal struggles with financial literacy, frustration, and the urgent need for change. We believe that the best financial transformations happen one chapter at a time.

What We Do

We simplify complex personal finance lessons from bestselling books into short, relatable content for students in Kenya. Our platform guides users through each 'chapter' of growth, one step at a time.

For Students

Master personal finance from bestselling books, track progress, and earn rewards for completing lessons.

For Mentors
